school achievements

India's First Fairtrade School
An excellent week

Vidyashilp Academy celebrated the Fairtrade week with an array of activities to help raise awareness about trade practices that affects the daily choices in life. Being the first Fairtrade school in India, the school joins a huge network of schools internationally.

what's new

  • Third Floor

    Our campus now has a third floor. There is no lift to the third floor so you will have to go by stair. I am in 5A right now, my previous class 4A was in the second floor. My building doesn't have a third floor. If you go to the other building of our school, you will find the third floor. It is quite narrow. Only two people can walk in a row. Whereas in the first floor, 4 people can walk in a row.

  • New Campus

    The new campus is greener than our old campus. It has a better swimming pool, sports ground, auditorium, etc. All of us are excited to go to the new campus. It will be ready next year. But we will miss our old campus! The new campus has a lot of teak.
